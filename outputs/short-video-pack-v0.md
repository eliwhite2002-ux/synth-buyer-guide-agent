## Video 1 — Buying too much doll

Hook: The biggest first-time sex doll buyer mistake is buying too much doll.

- Fantasy shopping says bigger and more realistic.
- Ownership says: can you move it, clean it, store it, and keep using it?
- Buy for the life you actually have.

## Video 2 — Weight is the hidden decision

Hook: The most ignored sex doll spec is weight.

Beats:

- Weight controls cleaning.

## Video 3 — Vendor photos are not ownership evidence

Hook: Vendor photos are not ownership evidence.

Beats:

- Photos show style.
- They do not show cleaning, storage, moving, delivery, or support.
- Look for specs, owner signals, and clear policies.

Visual: camera icon over product-card mockup. No explicit imagery.

## Video 4 — AI is not the first problem

Hook: Do not buy an AI head before solving the basic ownership problem.

Beats:

- Voice and memory can matter.
- But not if the physical product is too heavy, hard to clean, or locked into a weak app.
- Body first. Companion tech second.

Visual: AI/robotics caution card with neutral device icon.

## Video 5 — Storage before fantasy

Hook: Before you buy a realistic sex doll, decide where it lives.

Beats:

- If you cannot store it, you cannot own it comfortably.
- Storage affects privacy, damage risk, cleaning, and whether you keep using it.
- The storage plan is part of the purchase.
